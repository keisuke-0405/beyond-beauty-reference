Sustainability Commitment

Sustainability is becoming increasingly important for all participants, including event exhibitors and visitors,
and for the entire market.

Through holding events, we can gain many opportunities to positively impact sustainable cities, communities, the environment, society, and the economy.

We aim to contribute to improving sustainability in the market by fully recognizing our responsibility and influence in holding events and expanding the network of industry stakeholders.

01

Market Support

Foundation Supporting Industry Growth

Promote the development of the beauty industry and create an environment where innovative products and services reach the market. Contribute to the revitalization of the entire industry through meeting places for exhibitors and visitors.

02

Environmental Consideration

Reduction of Emissions and Waste

Practice minimization of CO2 emissions and appropriate disposal of waste in venue operations. Reduce environmental load through the use of recyclable materials and efficient energy management.

03

Social Responsibility

Ethical Operation and Urban Contribution

Thoroughly implement transparent business operations and compliance with labor standards. Aim for coexistence of industry and society through contribution to the local community in Tokyo.

Informa Markets Initiatives

"BEYOND BEAUTY TOKYO" is part of Informa Markets and the Informa Group. Informa's sustainability focuses on the long-term impact on our customers, colleagues, the communities we work in, and the environment. Many of our stakeholders clearly state that sustainability is a necessary part of any event. We must verify that we are meeting those expectations and are convinced that being sustainable is the right thing to do. We also deeply recognize that we play a role in providing a place for the industry to promote sustainability, not just in holding events and producing related products and materials. This is an ongoing process for us, and we are exploring ideas on how we can improve and further develop in the future.
