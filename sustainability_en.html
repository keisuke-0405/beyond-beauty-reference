Our Sustainability Commitment

Driving Sustainable Change in Beauty.
Sustainability is no longer an option; it is essential for the future of our industry and our planet.

We believe that events can be powerful catalysts for positive change in communities, the environment, and the economy.

We are committed to leveraging our platform to promote sustainable practices and foster a responsible beauty ecosystem.

01

Market Support

Empowering the Market

We support the growth of a sustainable beauty industry by providing a platform for innovative, eco-friendly products and services to shine.

02

Environmental Stewardship

Reducing Our Footprint

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact through waste reduction, energy efficiency, and the use of sustainable materials in our event operations.

03

Social Responsibility

Ethical & Inclusive

We operate with transparency and integrity, ensuring fair labor practices and contributing positively to the local Tokyo community.

Informa Markets Initiatives

As part of the Informa Group, BEYOND BEAUTY TOKYO is aligned with a global sustainability strategy. We focus on long-term positive impacts for our customers, colleagues, and communities. We recognize our role not just as event organizers, but as facilitators of industry-wide sustainability. This is an ongoing journey, and we are constantly exploring new ways to improve.
